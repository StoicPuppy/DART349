Design

I want to use two different fonts for this website.

Markazi text will be my font of choice for titles and subtitles

Noto serif is a font I really like and I want to use it for all text the user will read.

Color palet

I want to make use of a variety of shades of black, white and gray to give the website a more contemporary and modernlook. I also want to use some shades of light brown/beige when writing about my dog Ringo's story. Ringo has a black, white and gray coat of fur with some light brown on his cheeks and legs.

Interaction

The user will be able to navigate different sections that include stories of dogs being rescued, documented research on the actions being taken by the government to cull dog populations, and actions by activist groups that are taking action to help solve the crisis and save dogs in a humane way.

The website will include a censored option for users that dont want to see disturbing content while they read. When doing my research I found a lot of website would surround their text with images of dead stray dogs. While the truth of the matter is that a large population of strays are being killed, it makes it harder for readers to consentrate on their reading as well as turns most people away when they see graphic content. I want to ensure that users are comfortable navigating and getting informed
